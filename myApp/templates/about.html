The problem we're solving

Every day, people leave hospitals holding documents they don't understand.

Lab results with reference ranges that mean nothing. Discharge papers written for other doctors. Prescriptions with no explanation of what they're actually for.

Most people do what they've always done — they guess, they worry, or they ask someone who also doesn't know. That gap causes real harm.

Delayed follow-up care

Patients who don't understand instructions miss follow-up appointments and medication schedules at significantly higher rates.

Unnecessary anxiety

Without context, even normal results feel alarming. The unknown is almost always scarier than the truth.

Preventable readmissions

Misunderstood discharge instructions are one of the leading causes of hospital readmission within 30 days.

Caregiver burden

Family members managing someone else's health carry this confusion alone — without training, without support.

What Aira is — and what it isn't

A medical document assistant. Not a diagnostic tool.

Aira reads lab reports, discharge papers, prescriptions, and clinical summaries — and explains them in plain language that patients and caregivers can actually use.

It does not tell you what's wrong with you. It does not replace your doctor.

"What Aira does is give you the understanding you need to have a better conversation with your doctor — to know which results need attention, to follow your discharge instructions correctly, to feel less alone in a complicated moment."

01 — Context-aware

Aira reads who's asking, not just what they're asking

A question about the same lab result gets a different response depending on whether you're asking for yourself, for your elderly mother, or to share with your care team. No settings to configure. Aira adapts from the first message.

02 — Tone as a feature

Calm, clear, and honest — even about what needs attention

Medical anxiety is real. Aira is designed to never alarm unnecessarily — while still being direct about what matters. The language is human, not clinical. The delivery is warm, not robotic.

03 — Clarity with direction

Every response ends with what to do next

Understanding without direction isn't enough. Every Aira response includes practical next steps: what to ask your doctor, what to monitor, what's routine and what isn't. It's information you can actually act on.

04 — Built for everyone

Designed for real families in real moments

Whether you're a caregiver managing a parent's health from across the country, a patient processing a new diagnosis, or a family member trying to understand what just happened — Aira meets you where you are, in language that feels human.

Our approach to privacy

Privacy isn't a feature.
It's foundational.

Medical information is among the most sensitive data that exists. We treat it that way — not because regulations require it, but because the people using Aira deserve nothing less.

Your documents are processed in encrypted, isolated sessions. They are never stored beyond your active conversation unless you explicitly choose to save them.

Our honest position

We're a team building toward full healthcare data compliance. Our infrastructure is built to privacy-first standards from day one. We're working through the operational requirements — audit controls, BAAs, access logging — that formal HIPAA certification requires. We won't claim it before we've earned it.

End-to-end encrypted

All documents encrypted in transit and at rest on AWS infrastructure.

Never sold or shared

Your data is never sold to third parties or shared with advertisers — ever.

Never used for training

We do not use your conversations or documents to train AI models.

AWS secure infrastructure

Built on AWS with isolated, private processing sessions per user.

Zero-retention sessions

Documents are cleared at session end unless you explicitly save them.

BAA-ready architecture

Infrastructure built to support BAA agreements as we scale to enterprise.

Who we built this for

Three people kept us up at night.

Every design decision in Aira traces back to one of these three people — and the moment where clear information would have changed everything for them.

The caregiver

Managing someone else's health is exhausting and often confusing — especially when the documents arrive in language designed for clinicians, not families. Aira helps you stay on top of what matters without needing a medical background.

The patient navigating something new

A first diagnosis, an unexpected result, a discharge after something scary. Aira gives you a starting point — something to hold onto before you can talk to your doctor. Clarity in the hardest moments.

The family far away

When someone you love is sick and you can't be there, Aira helps you understand what's happening and what questions to ask. So distance doesn't mean being left in the dark.
