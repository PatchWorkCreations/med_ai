Share your summary safely

Two easy ways to share results with family or your clinician.

Option A: Download a PDF

  1. Go back to the chat.
  2. Generate a summary (or upload a file).
  3. Click “Download PDF.”
Download current summary

Tip: PDF is great for printing or attaching to email/portal messages.

Option B: Share as a link

  1. Make a summary in the chat.
  2. Click “Create shareable link.”
  3. Send the link to your caregiver or clinician.
Open link

Private by default: only people with the link can view.

Privacy & safety

  • We don’t post anything publicly; shared links are unlisted and expire automatically.
  • Remove personal identifiers from uploads if you’re concerned (name, MRN, address).
  • This is for education; it’s not a medical diagnosis.
