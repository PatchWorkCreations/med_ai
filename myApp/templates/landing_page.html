About NeuroMed AI

NeuroMed AI helps patients and caregivers understand medical records by converting complex medical documents into clear, easy-to-understand language. Our AI-powered platform provides personalized summaries in multiple tones (Plain, Caregiver, Faith-based, Clinical, Bilingual) and supports over 40 languages.

How We Use Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we use your Google account information to:

  • Email address: To create and manage your account, send you important updates about your medical summaries, and provide customer support.
  • Name: To personalize your experience and display your name in your account dashboard.
  • Profile information: To enhance your account setup and provide a better user experience.

We do not share your Google account information with third parties. Your medical data and summaries are stored securely and are only accessible to you. We use your information solely to provide and improve our medical document summarization services.
