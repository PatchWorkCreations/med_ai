1. Legal & Compliance Framework

  • HIPAA (U.S.): Safeguards for Protected Health Information (PHI) across storage, transmission, and access.
  • GDPR (EU): Consent, transparency, and user rights (access, deletion, portability).

3. Consent & Transparency

  • Explicit consent before summarizing or sharing (clear, plain language).
  • Audit trails for every access, summary, or data action.
  • Right to be forgotten (GDPR): ability to delete data upon request.

4. Operational Safeguards

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with cloud providers.
  • Training & Policies for staff/contractors (HIPAA/GDPR).
  • Incident Response Plan with GDPR 72-hour notification.

5. Technical Best Practices for AI

  • De-identification: strip names, DOBs, identifiers before processing.
  • Secure sandboxing: prevent PHI from leaving private endpoints.
  • Model guardrails: block recalling/reusing personal data in outputs.

6. User Trust Features

  • Patient & Caregiver dashboards: show exactly what data is used.
  • Export/Delete: self-service data control anytime.
  • Family access: secure portals over email attachments.
